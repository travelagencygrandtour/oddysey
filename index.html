Destinations

Embark on a journey to the world's most captivating destinations with Grand Odyssey Escapes and Tours. Our curated selection of destinations offers a tapestry of experiences, from sun-soaked beaches to culturally rich landscapes. Explore the wonders that await you:

Batanes

Discover the raw and untouched beauty of Batanes, where rolling hills, dramatic cliffs, and serene landscapes create a picture-perfect destination. Batanes is a gem for travelers seeking tranquility and natural wonders.

Bohol

Explore the Chocolate Hills, encounter adorable tarsiers, and relax on the pristine beaches of Bohol. This island province in the Philippines is a nature lover's paradise, rich in biodiversity and natural wonders.

Boracay

Relax on the powdery white sands of Boracay, known for its vibrant nightlife, water activities, and stunning sunsets. Experience the perfect blend of relaxation and excitement on this renowned Philippine island.
